Support Message
Please be informed that all donations are intended to support the sustainability of the fan club — both as a club and as a community.

As a united family, we contribute to causes such as supporting orphanages, children without parents, and individuals with disabilities. We kindly ask for any support you can offer — no matter how small — because every contribution counts.

Within our community, we stand by each other during difficult times, including the loss of loved ones, and we also support each other’s businesses in every little way possible.

All of this is made possible through your generosity. At the end of the day, we are united by one cause: growth — together.
